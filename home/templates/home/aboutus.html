About Us

Mission And Values

The AWS Cloud Club aims to empower students and technology enthusiasts by providing comprehensive learning opportunities and hands-on experiences with AWS cloud technologies. We foster a supportive community for collaboration and innovation, encouraging members to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact. Our club also focuses on career development by offering networking, mentorship, and guidance to help members succeed in cloud computing and related fields.

Get Answer To Your Most Asked Questions

What is the AWS Cloud Club in Nepal?

The AWS Cloud Club in Nepal is a community-driven organization dedicated to educating and empowering students and tech enthusiasts through learning opportunities, hands-on experiences, and networking events centered around AWS cloud computing.

Who can join the AWS Cloud Club in Nepal?

The club is open to all students and technology enthusiasts in Nepal who are interested in exploring AWS cloud technologies, regardless of their experience level.

How do I become a member of the AWS Cloud Club in Nepal?

You can join by attending our events, participating in our activities, and signing up through our online registration form available on the club's website or at our meetings.

What kind of events does the AWS Cloud Club in Nepal organize?

We organize a variety of events, including hands-on workshops, technical talks by industry experts, certification study sessions, and hackathons to foster learning and innovation.

Do I need any prior experience with AWS to join the club?

No prior experience is required. Our events are designed to cater to beginners as well as those with more advanced knowledge of AWS technologies.

How can the club help me with AWS certification?

We provide study groups, access to resources, and mentorship from certified AWS professionals to support members in preparing for AWS certification exams.

Are there any membership fees for the AWS Cloud Club in Nepal?

Membership is free, but some events may have a small fee to cover materials or special guests.

How can I stay updated on club activities and events?

Follow us on our social media platforms, subscribe to our newsletter, and check our website regularly for the latest updates and event information.

Can I suggest topics or speakers for club events?

Yes! We encourage members to share their ideas and suggestions for topics or speakers they would like to see featured in our events.

How can the AWS Cloud Club in Nepal benefit my career?

By joining the club, you'll gain valuable skills, hands-on experience, and networking opportunities with industry professionals, which can enhance your career prospects and open doors to new opportunities.

How do I contact the club for more information?

You can reach us via email at awscloudclub.nepal@gmail.com or connect with us on our social media channels for more information and inquiries.
